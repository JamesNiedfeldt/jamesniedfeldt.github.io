Initiative Tracker

Initiative Tracker is a Java program meant to make Dungeons and Dragons 5th Edition simpler to play by providing a system of tracking battles. It uses JavaFX to create an interface for dungeon masters to add players and enemies, track turn order, and modify player stats quickly and easily.

Some features of Initiative Tracker:

  • Edit relevant character/enemy stats easily as needed. HP and conditions can be modified on the fly.
  • Turn order is determined automatically by supplying dexterity and initiative scores, and current/upcoming turns are tracked.
  • Enemies can be added in bulk by supplying base stat blocks.
  • Encounters can be saved and loaded for future use.
